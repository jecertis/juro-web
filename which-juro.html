Two products called Juro — which one are you looking for?

If you landed here looking for contract lifecycle management software, you want juro.com — a separate company that helps legal and ops teams manage contracts. JeCertis (trading as Juro at jurocompliant.com) builds compliance scanning software. The two products solve different problems and have no relationship.

What JeCertis actually does:

  • Scans your public-facing surface and codebase for regulatory obligation markers. The scanner reads privacy policies, consent implementations, and data handling disclosures against GDPR, India's DPDP Act 2023, and DORA requirements — and reports what it detects versus what regulations require. Absence of a finding is not a compliance verdict; it is a gap signal.
  • Produces signed, deterministic artefacts. Every scan generates a cryptographically signed output tied to a specific target, a specific ruleset hash, and a specific timestamp. The artefact is customer-held — no infrastructure state leaves your perimeter. Any auditor or counterparty can independently re-verify the finding without trusting JeCertis.
  • Runs non-custodially, inside your perimeter for deep scans. The Tier 3 agent deploys inside your VPC under a read-only IAM role. It maps data flows, IAM policies, encryption state, and audit log coverage locally. The only outbound call is a SHA-256 fingerprint of the signed bundle to a public notary log — no cloud state, no PII, no infrastructure metadata leaves your environment.

Who this is for:

DPOs, CTOs, and compliance leads at India-facing SaaS companies managing DPDP obligations — and at companies with EU operations managing GDPR Article 28 processor obligations or DORA ICT risk requirements. Particularly useful when a signed DPA or processor agreement exists but no technical evidence backs the obligations stated in it.

The Tier 1 surface scanner is public and requires no account. It scans your domain's public-facing posture — consent behaviour, privacy policy markers, regulatory language — and produces a finding report with specific rule citations.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between Juro and Jurocompliant?

Juro (juro.com) is a contract lifecycle management platform that helps legal teams draft and manage contracts. Jurocompliant (jurocompliant.com) is a non-custodial compliance scanner that produces cryptographically signed evidence of GDPR, DPDP, and DORA compliance. They are separate companies with no relationship.

Which Juro is the compliance tool for GDPR and DPDP?

Jurocompliant (jurocompliant.com), built by JeCertis, is the compliance scanner. It scans infrastructure and public-facing surfaces for GDPR, India's DPDP Act 2023, and DORA compliance markers, and produces signed, verifiable artefacts.

Is Jurocompliant the same as Juro contract management?

No. Juro.com and Jurocompliant.com are unrelated products. Juro.com handles contract management. Jurocompliant.com handles compliance evidence — scanning for GDPR, DPDP, and DORA obligations and producing cryptographically signed scan artefacts.
